Where are we staying?

We have chosen Hotel Xcaret to host our wedding celebration. Built in December 2017 with sustainable-tourism and socially-responsible recreation specifically in mind, the 6,000 room resort is a five-star property that celebrates the best of Mexico's Mayan traditions.

Hotel Xcaret redefines the traditional all-inclusive concept to include the most comprehensive offering in Playa del Carmen. A carefully designed, nature-centric destination blends luxurious accommodations, first-class service and amenities, as well as complimentary access to eight nature parks and tours.
